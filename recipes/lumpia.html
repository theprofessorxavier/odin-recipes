Lumpia

Description

Lumpia are Filipino spring rolls, a popular and festive treat. These crispy, deep-fried rolls consist of a thin wrapper encasing a savory filling typically made with ground pork, chopped vegetables like carrots, onions, and cabbage, and seasoned with garlic, ginger, and soy sauce. The golden-brown, crunchy exterior gives way to a flavorful interior, often complemented by a sweet and sour dipping sauce. A staple at Filipino celebrations and gatherings, lumpia are enjoyed as appetizers or snacks and represent a beloved and shareable comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Ground Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Seasonings
  • Binder

Steps

  1. Prepare the Filling
  2. Add Vegetables and Seasonings
  3. Bind the Filling
  4. Wrap the Lumpia
  5. Fry the Lumpia
  6. Drain and Serve
